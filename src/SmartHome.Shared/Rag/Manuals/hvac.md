# Aria HVAC Air Handler

## Replacing the air filter
Replace every 90 days, or every 30 days with pets. Power down at the wall switch
before opening the filter door. Slide out the old filter, noting the airflow arrow.
Insert the new filter with the arrow pointing toward the blower. Close the door,
restore power, and reset the filter timer in the Aria Home app.

## Filter size
16x25x1 inch MERV 11.
