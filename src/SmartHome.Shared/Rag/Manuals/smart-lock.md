# Aria Smart Deadbolt (model DL-10)

## Guest PIN management
Guest PINs are issued and revoked from the Aria Home app under Devices > Front
Door > Guest Access, not on the keypad itself. Each guest PIN can be limited to a
date range or a recurring weekly window. The homeowner's own PIN is shown on the
owner's contact card and should be rotated if a guest PIN's date range is ever
left open-ended.

## Battery replacement
The DL-10 takes 4x AA batteries, rated for about 8 months of typical use. Replace
all 4 at once, not individually — mixing old and new cells is the most common
cause of the lock reporting "low battery" again within days of a replacement.

## Safety warning
The DL-10 has a mechanical key override behind the faceplate for fire-code
compliance; do not remove or epoxy over this cylinder even if you only ever use
the keypad, as some jurisdictions require a manual egress method.

## Specifications
Bluetooth range: approximately 12 metres indoors. Auto-lock delay: configurable
from 0 (off) to 5 minutes after the door closes. Operating temperature: -20°C to
50°C, rated for exterior doors.

## Troubleshooting: keypad lights up but the deadbolt doesn't move
A keypad that lights up and beeps but doesn't throw the bolt usually means the
motor is straining against a misaligned strike plate, not a dead battery — batteries
that are too weak to drive the motor typically fail to light the keypad at all.
Check the door latches smoothly by hand with the deadbolt retracted before assuming
the lock itself is faulty.

## Troubleshooting: lock reports low battery right after changing batteries
See Battery replacement above — this is almost always a mixed old/new battery set,
not a defective unit. Replace all 4 cells together with the same brand and check
again before contacting support.

## Troubleshooting: guest PIN stopped working before its listed expiry date
Guest PINs are capped at 10 active codes at once; adding an 11th silently
deactivates the oldest one rather than showing an error. Check Devices > Front
Door > Guest Access for the full active list if a PIN stops working unexpectedly.

## Support
Warranty is 24 months from purchase, excluding the mechanical key cylinder, which
is covered by the door hardware manufacturer, not Aria. The guest door PIN
mentioned on the owner's contact card is separate from the app-issued guest PINs
above and cannot be changed from the app.
