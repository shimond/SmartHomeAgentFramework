# Aria Bean-to-Cup Coffee Machine

## Descaling
Descale the machine every 2 months or when the descale light blinks amber.
Use 100 ml of food-grade descaling solution mixed with 900 ml of fresh water.
Pour the mixture into the water tank. Hold STEAM and POWER together for 5 seconds
to enter descale mode; the light turns solid amber. Place a 1.5 litre container
under the spout and press START. The cycle runs about 25 minutes.
Afterward, rinse the tank and run two water-only cycles before brewing coffee again.

## Cleaning the brew group
Remove the brew group weekly via the release tab on the right side. Rinse under
warm water without detergent and let it air dry.
