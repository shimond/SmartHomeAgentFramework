# Aria Smart Thermostat (model TH-100)

## Factory reset
A factory reset clears the schedule, Wi-Fi credentials and paired devices.
Press and hold the dial for 10 seconds until the screen shows "RESET?". Rotate to
select YES and press once to confirm. The thermostat reboots and shows a setup QR
code after about 30 seconds. Re-pair from the Aria Home app under Devices > Add.

## Temperature range
Accepts targets between 10°C and 30°C in 0.5° steps.

## Safety warning
Do not mount the thermostat within 1 metre of a heat source (fireplace, direct sun
through a window, or a lamp) — it will read the local heat and misjudge the whole
house's temperature, potentially leaving the system running far longer than needed.

## Specifications
Display: 3.5 inch e-ink, always-on. Power: wired C-wire preferred; battery backup
(2x AAA) lasts about 4 months if the C-wire is unavailable. Wi-Fi: 2.4GHz only.

## Troubleshooting: app can't find the thermostat during setup
This is almost always a 5GHz network issue — the TH-100 only supports 2.4GHz
Wi-Fi. If your router broadcasts a single combined SSID, temporarily create a
separate 2.4GHz-only network name for setup, then the thermostat will reconnect
automatically to the main network afterward.

## Troubleshooting: schedule changes don't stick after a day or two
If a manually-set temperature keeps reverting, a schedule is still active and
overriding it — manual adjustments only hold "until next scheduled change" unless
you explicitly choose "Hold Permanently" on the dial menu after adjusting.

## Troubleshooting: screen is completely blank
Check the C-wire connection at the wall plate first; a loose C-wire causes the
unit to fall back to battery power, and depleted AAAs will blank the display
entirely rather than showing a low-battery warning. Swapping the batteries fixes
this in most cases without needing a reset.

## Troubleshooting: displayed temperature seems wrong compared to a room thermometer
This is expected if the thermostat is near a vent, doorway, or direct sunlight — see
the Safety warning above on mounting location. Recalibration under MENU > 4 >
Calibrate can offset the reading by up to ±3°C if relocating isn't possible.

## Support
Warranty is 24 months from purchase. Contact Aria support via the app's Help tab;
have the model number (TH-100) and the serial number from the battery
compartment ready.
