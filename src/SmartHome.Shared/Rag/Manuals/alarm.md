# Aria Home Alarm Panel (model AH-200)

## Resetting the master code
Enter the current master code, press MENU, then 9. Enter the new 4-6 digit code,
press OK, then re-enter to confirm. If the code is lost, hold RESET for 15 seconds
to enter installer mode; this also disarms the system.

## Arming modes
HOME (perimeter only) and AWAY (perimeter and motion).

## Safety warning
The AH-200 backup battery contains lithium. Do not puncture, crush, or expose it to
open flame. If the panel smells of burning plastic or the case feels hot to the
touch, disconnect mains power at the breaker and stop using the unit immediately.

## Specifications
Mains: 100-240V AC adapter. Backup battery: 7.2V lithium, lasts approximately 18
hours on outage. Wireless range to door/window sensors: up to 60 metres
line-of-sight. Operating temperature: 0-40°C.

## Troubleshooting: panel beeps every 30 seconds
A repeating single beep with no display message almost always means the backup
battery is low, not that a sensor is faulted. Replace the battery (see Specifications
for the part number) and the beeping stops within a minute of reinsertion.

## Troubleshooting: keypad shows LOW BATT
This refers to a door or window sensor's coin-cell battery, not the panel's own
backup battery. The keypad names the affected zone when you press STATUS. Replace
the CR2032 cell in that sensor.

## Troubleshooting: false alarms from pets
Motion zones set to AWAY mode can trigger on cats and dogs over roughly 18kg. Switch
the affected zone's sensitivity to "Pet Immune" under MENU > 7 > Zone Setup, or
arm in HOME mode when a pet is home alone, which only watches doors and windows.

## Support
Warranty is 24 months from purchase against manufacturing defects; it does not
cover battery wear, water damage, or self-installation errors. Contact Aria support
through the Aria Home app's Help tab for panel replacements.
